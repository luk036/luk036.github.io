## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Nerd Fonts** are essential for CLI tools 👨‍💻
2. Use **tmux** or **screen** for session management
3. **Aliases** save time:
   ```bash
   alias ll='ls -la'
   alias gs='git status'
   ```
