Earth is Not Flat
A brief demonstration of known facts
🌍
Earth is an oblate spheroid
— slightly flattened at the poles
and bulging at the equator.
⛵
Ships disappear hull-first
over the horizon —
consistent only with a curved surface.
⭐
Different constellations
are visible from the northern vs.
southern hemisphere.
Trust Evidence
Science is not a belief system —
it's a method for finding out what's true.
