
### Projective Geometry

- Point/line
- Incidence
- Projectivity
- Harmonic relation, harmonic conjugate
- Polarity, conic
- Fudamental cones
- No left/right, up/down, inside/outside
- Quadrance/Spread

### Caley-Keith Geometry

- Pole/polar
- Orthogonality
- Involution
- Parallel
- Middle Point, angle bisector
- Hyperbolic, Elliptic, Euclidean
- Rational trigonometry
  Archimedes formula, Ar(q1, q2, q3) = (q1 + q2 + q3)^2 - 2 (q1^2 + q2^2 + q3^2) = 0

### Euclidean Geometry

- Similar triangle
- Circle, circle center
- Ptolemy Theorem
- Circle Inversion
